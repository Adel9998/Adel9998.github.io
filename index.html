Prabowo Subianto

TNI Lieutenant General H. Prabowo Subianto Djojohadikusumo is a politician, businessman and high-ranking Indonesian military officer of Javanese Banyumasan-Minahasa descent, because his father's family comes from Kebumen and Banyumas and his mother comes from Manado..

About Prabowo

As General Chair of the Gerindra Party, Prabowo Subianto together with Hatta Rajasa, ran as candidates for the 7th President of Indonesia in the 2014 general election, but were defeated by the pair Joko Widodo and Jusuf Kalla. He ran for president in the 2019 Indonesian Presidential general election, paired with Sandiaga Uno. At the National Meeting of the Great Indonesia Movement Party on August 13 2022 in Sentul, Prabowo accepted his party's nomination to run for president in the 2024 Indonesian Presidential Election.

Born in Jakarta, Prabowo's childhood as the son of economist Soemitro Djojohadikoesoemo was spent abroad with his parents. Because of this experience, Prabowo is fluent in Indonesian, French, German, English and Dutch. His interest in the military world was influenced by his uncle, Soebianto Djojohadikusumo, who died in the Battle of Lengkong. After graduating from the Army Academy in Magelang in 1974[4] as a second lieutenant, he became one of the youngest operations commanders in Army history when he led the Nanggala Team operations in East Timor. His career skyrocketed after serving as Deputy Commander of the Counter-Terror Detachment at the Special Forces Command in 1983. In 1996, Prabowo was appointed as Commander General of the corps. While in office, he led the operation to free hostages in Mapenduma. When President Soeharto, his father-in-law, fell in May 1998, Prabowo was serving as Commander of the Strategic Reserve Command.

Prospective presidential candidate Prabowo Subianto on Sunday officially announced that President Joko “Jokowi” Widodo's eldest son, Gibran Rakabuming Raka, would be his running mate in the 2024 election.

Prabowo himself has stated that he will run for the 2024 presidential election. His candidacy has received support from Gerindra, Golkar, Democrat, PAN, Garuda, PBB and Gelora. Prabowo's candidacy in the 2024 presidential election does not require him to leave his ministerial post. Based on the Constitutional Court decision number 68?PUU-XX/2022, ministerial officials may take part in the presidential election without having to resign. The Constitutional Court's decision also changes the provisions of article 170 paragraph (1) of the Election Law. In previous regulations, state officials had to resign from office when running for president. Read the CNN Indonesia article "Advancing for 2024 Presidential Candidate, Prabowo Subianto Gets the Blessing of Jokowi".

KPU Commissioner Idham Holik stated that the three presidential and vice presidential candidate pairs who had registered with the KPU "met the requirements and were declared presidential and vice presidential candidate pairs for the 2024 simultaneous elections". Previously, three pairs of presidential and vice presidential candidates had registered with the KPU to compete in the 2024 presidential election, namely the pair Anies Baswedan-Muhaimin Iskandar, Ganjar Pranowo-Mahfud MD, and Prabowo Subianto-Gibran Rakabuming Raka. Before being appointed as a presidential and vice presidential candidate pair by the KPU, they must go through a verification process. This process is important to ensure that all presidential and vice presidential candidates meet the requirements and criteria set by the KPU before being announced as official participants in the 2024 presidential election.
